The Core Connecting Idea
Article 356 was designed as a constitutional safety net ‚Äî a last resort (per Ambedkar) when state governance collapses. But it was chronically misused for political purposes. The S.R. Bommai judgment (1994) fundamentally changed this by making President's Rule subject to judicial review, requiring objective material, and insisting that majority must be tested on the floor ‚Äî not guessed by governors. The 44th Amendment removed immunity. Today, Art. 356 is far harder to misuse.
