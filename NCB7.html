🧠 Big Picture — India's Complete Governance Architecture
🏴
The Connected Governance Insight
NITI Aayog designs policy → Home Guards maintain internal order → Consumer Commissions protect citizen rights → ED and DRI enforce financial law. Together they represent India's complete governance architecture: Policy + Security + Rights + Enforcement. Each body has a precise ministry, a precise law, and a precise role — UPSC tests all three dimensions.
🧠 NITI = Policy 👮 HG = Security 🛒 Courts = Rights 💰 ED/DRI = Enforcement 🐚 Gandhi Prize = Recognition 📊 Ministry Map = Trap Buster
