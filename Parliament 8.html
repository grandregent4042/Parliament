‚ö†Ô∏è Trap 1 ‚Äî RS & Money Bills
RS CANNOT amend a Money Bill ‚Äî only recommend. Deemed passed if RS doesn't return within 14 days. Speaker's certification is FINAL and unchallengeable.
‚ö†Ô∏è Trap 2 ‚Äî Joint Sitting Exclusions
NO Joint Sitting for Money Bills. NO Joint Sitting for Constitutional Amendment Bills. Only resolves deadlocks on Ordinary + Financial Bills.
‚ö†Ô∏è Trap 3 ‚Äî Art 249 Majority
2/3rd of members PRESENT & VOTING ‚Äî NOT 2/3rd of total membership. This distinction is a frequent MCQ trap.
‚ö†Ô∏è Trap 4 ‚Äî VP vs President Removal
President impeachment ‚Üí can start in EITHER House. VP removal ‚Üí ONLY RS initiates (Art 67). LS only concurs for VP.
‚ö†Ô∏è Trap 5 ‚Äî No-Confidence Motion
ONLY Lok Sabha can pass No-Confidence Motion. Art 75 ‚Äî ministers collectively responsible to LS alone. RS motion does NOT topple the government.
‚ö†Ô∏è Trap 6 ‚Äî Emergency & LS Dissolution
If LS is dissolved, RS ALONE can approve Emergency proclamation. Stays effective until new LS. Applies to all 3 emergencies (352, 356, 360).
‚ö†Ô∏è Trap 7 ‚Äî All India Services
RS ALONE creates All India Services (Art 312). IAS, IPS ‚Äî created by RS resolution. LS has absolutely NO role in this process.
‚ö†Ô∏è Trap 8 ‚Äî Permanent vs Dissolved
RS is PERMANENT ‚Äî can NEVER be dissolved. LS CAN be dissolved by the President on PM's advice. This drives the RS emergency power.

The Core Connecting Idea
India's Parliament has a deliberate asymmetry. Lok Sabha = democratic mandate + financial power. Rajya Sabha = federal balance + institutional continuity. Together they ensure both majority will and federal safeguard. India rejected the US equal-state model ‚Äî choosing population-based representation: unity over symmetry.
