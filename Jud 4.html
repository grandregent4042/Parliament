The Core Connecting Idea
India's judiciary extends from the Supreme Court down to Gram Nyayalayas. Subordinate courts are under HC control; ADR mechanisms reduce court burden; Tribunals provide specialist justice but remain subject to HC judicial review (L. Chandra Kumar, 1997).
NALSA + Lok Adalat = Free + Fast justice for the poor. 42nd Amendment = foundation of the tribunal system.
