The Core Connecting Idea
India's good governance triangle rests on three pillars: CVC catches corruption, CIC enforces transparency at centre, SIC enforces it at states. All three are statutory (not constitutional), share civil court powers, and are appointed through committees involving PM/CM + Leader of Opposition. The key differentiator is the 3rd member of each appointment committee.
