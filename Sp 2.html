The Core Connecting Idea
Articles 371‚Äì371J prove that Indian federalism is not uniform ‚Äî it is adaptive. Added through amendments (not original Constitution), these provisions show that the Constitution is a living document that accommodates diversity through asymmetric federalism. Each provision reflects a unique historical, political, or cultural challenge.
