The Core Idea ‚Äî What Makes SC Supreme
The Supreme Court is not merely a court ‚Äî it interprets the Constitution (Art 147), advises the President (Art 143), grants special leave from any court (Art 136), ensures complete justice (Art 142), and its law binds all courts (Art 141). Combined with Art 144's enforcement power, the SC is the apex constitutional authority of India ‚Äî regulating its own procedure, designating lawyers, and shaping constitutional jurisprudence through 12 key doctrines.
