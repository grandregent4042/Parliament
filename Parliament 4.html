The Core Connecting Logic
Parliament's session lifecycle flows from Presidential summoning ‚Üí sittings ‚Üí adjournment ‚Üí prorogation ‚Üí recess ‚Üí re-summoning. The critical asymmetry: Lok Sabha can die (dissolution) but Rajya Sabha lives forever ‚Äî this explains ALL lapsing rules. Bills tied to LS fate lapse; bills anchored in RS survive. Voting requires only a simple majority of present-and-voting members; the Speaker is the tiebreaker, not a voter.
