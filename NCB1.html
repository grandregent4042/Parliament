Exam Bombs ‚Äî Get Every Single One Right

‚ö†Ô∏è High-Frequency Mistake Zones

Statutory, NOT Constitutional

Lokpal is NOT in the Constitution. Created by law. Biggest trap in exams.

Multi-Member Body

NOT single-member. 1 Chairperson + up to 8 Members. Total = max 9 persons.

50% Must Be Judicial

Half of all members must be judicial (SC judge / HC Chief Justice). Mandatory minimum.

50% Reserved Categories

SC/ST/OBC/Minorities/Women must form at least 50% of members.

Minimum Age = 45 Years

Not 35, not 50, not 40. Exactly 45 years minimum for any member.

Term = 5 Years or Age 70

Whichever is earlier. No reappointment after the term ends.

PM IS Under Lokpal

PM is covered ‚Äî but national security, defence, atomic energy excluded.

Inquiry Wing = 60 Days

Inquiry Wing must submit report within 60 days. Very specific fact.

Civil Court Powers

Lokpal has powers of a civil court ‚Äî summon witnesses, demand documents, seize.

The Core Connecting Idea
Democracy needs five pillars of accountability: Elections (ECI) + Finance (CAG) + Recruitment (UPSC) + Legal Advice (AG) + Anti-Corruption (Lokpal).
Lokpal is the last and newest pillar ‚Äî a statutory, multi-member, quasi-judicial body with civil court powers. It watches over the executive ‚Äî including the Prime Minister himself ‚Äî but is NOT in the Constitution. Statutory but powerful.
