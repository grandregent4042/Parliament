‚ùì Is Whip a constitutional provision?
‚ùå NO ‚Äî it's entirely convention-based, borrowed from Westminster. Not in Constitution.
‚ùì Is 106th CAA (Women Reservation) in effect?
‚ùå NOT YET ‚Äî awaits next delimitation. Passed but not operative as of 2023.
‚ùì Who appoints Secretary-General of Parliament?

‚ùå NO ‚Äî it EXTENDED SC/ST reservation to 2030 while removing Anglo-Indian nomination (Art 331).
‚ùì How are election disputes challenged?
‚úÖ ONLY via election petition after results (Ponnuswami, 1952). No court writ during election.
