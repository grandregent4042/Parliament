The Core Connecting Idea
State Legislature = Governor + Assembly (mandatory) + Council (optional in 6 states). Assembly is directly elected, powerful, and dominant ‚Äî especially in finance. Council is advisory, permanent, and delays. Speaker/Chairman manage procedures; Governor is constitutional head. Anti-defection = Speaker/Chairman, not Governor. The entire system mirrors Parliament but Assembly is always supreme within states.
