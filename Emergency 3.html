The Core Connecting Idea
Three types of emergencies ‚Äî Security, Political, Financial ‚Äî form India's constitutional safety net. 352 is most powerful (affects FRs), 356 is most used (100+ times), 360 is most unique (never used).
Remember: Only 352 needs Special Majority. Only 356 has a 3-year cap. Only 360 cuts judges' salaries.
