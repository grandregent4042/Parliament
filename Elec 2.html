The Core Connecting Idea
India's election architecture flows in one clean story: Census triggers Delimitation (Art 82/170) ‚Üí RPA 1950 builds the structure (rolls, seats, voters) ‚Üí RPA 1951 runs the game (conduct, disqualify, disputes).
The key insight: seats are frozen till 2026 but boundaries can change ‚Äî and Delimitation orders are final law, unchallengeable in court.
