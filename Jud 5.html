The Core Connecting Idea
India's judiciary evolved from being a passive interpreter to an active guardian. Judicial Review is the power; Activism is how it's used; Restraint is the balance; PIL is the public access tool.
The debate between Activism and Overreach defines modern constitutional jurisprudence in India.
